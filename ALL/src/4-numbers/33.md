------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Numbers 33:1

------------------------------------------------------------------------

¹ These are the travels of the children of Israel who went out from the
land of Egypt by their armies by the hand of Moses and Aaron.

[image]

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Numbers 33:2

------------------------------------------------------------------------

² And Moses wrote their stops for their travels by YHWH’s word, and these
are their travels and their stops.

[image]

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Numbers 33:3

------------------------------------------------------------------------

³ And they traveled from Rameses in the first month on the fifteenth day
of the first month. On the day after the Passover, the children of
Israel went out with a high hand before the eyes of all Egypt.

[image]

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Numbers 33:4

------------------------------------------------------------------------

⁴ And the Egyptians were burying those whom YHWH had struck among them:
every firstborn. And YHWH had made judgments on their gods.

[image]

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Numbers 33:5

------------------------------------------------------------------------

⁵ And the children of Israel traveled from Rameses and camped in Succoth.

[image]

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Numbers 33:6

------------------------------------------------------------------------

⁶ And they traveled from Succoth and camped in Etham, which is at the
edge of the wilderness.

[image]

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Numbers 33:7

------------------------------------------------------------------------

is in front of Baal-Zephon: and they camped in front of Migdol.

[image]

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Numbers 33:8

------------------------------------------------------------------------

⁸ And they traveled from before Pi-hahiroth and passed through the sea to
the wilderness. And they went three days’ journey in the wilderness of
Etham and camped in Marah.

[image]

## Numbers 33:50

------------------------------------------------------------------------

⁵⁰ And YHWH spoke to Moses in the plains of Moab by the Jordan toward
Jericho, saying,

[image]

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Numbers 33:51

------------------------------------------------------------------------

⁵¹“Speak to the children of Israel, and you shall say to them: When you
cross the Jordan to the land of Canaan,

[image]

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Numbers 33:52

------------------------------------------------------------------------

⁵² you shall dispossess all the residents of the land in front of you,
and destroy all their carved figures, and you shall destroy all their
molten images and demolish all their high places.

[image]

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Numbers 33:53

------------------------------------------------------------------------

⁵³ And you shall take possession of the land and live in it, because I’ve
given the land to you to possess it.

[image]

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Numbers 33:54

------------------------------------------------------------------------

⁵⁴ And you shall give legacies of the land by lot, by your families: for
the large you shall make its legacy larger, and for the small you shall
make its legacy smaller. Wherever the lot indicates for it shall belong
to it. You shall give legacies to the tribes of your fathers.

[image]

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Numbers 33:55

------------------------------------------------------------------------

⁵⁵ And if you don’t dispossess the residents of the land in front of you,
then those whom you leave of them will become sticks in your eyes and
thorns in your sides, and they’ll afflict you on the land in which
you’re living.

[image]

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Numbers 33:56

------------------------------------------------------------------------

⁵⁶ And it will be that, as I meant to do to them, I’ll do to you!”
