## Numbers 29:1

------------------------------------------------------------------------

29

¹“And in the seventh month on the first of the month you shall have a
holy assembly. You shall not do any act of work. You shall have a day of
horn-blasting.

[image]

---

Footnote 29:1. seventh month on the first of the month. This is the holiday that
later came to be known as Rosh Hashanah, the New Year. It is never
called by that name in the Torah, where it is rather counted as the
seventh month.
