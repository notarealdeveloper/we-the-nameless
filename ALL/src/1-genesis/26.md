## Genesis 26:4

------------------------------------------------------------------------

⁴ and I’ll multiply your seed like the stars of the skies and give to
your seed all these lands, and all the nations of the earth will be
blessed through your seed

---

Footnote 26:4. all the nations of the earth will be blessed through your seed.
This prediction was made to Abraham in his first communication from God.
It was repeated to him in Isaac’s presence at the Aqedah. Now God gives
the same message to Isaac himself. Now that Isaac has grown up and had
children himself, the renewal of the message confirms that it is through
his descendants that this is to occur.

## Genesis 26:12

------------------------------------------------------------------------

¹² And Isaac planted seed in that land, and he harvested a hundredfold in
that year. And YHWH blessed him,

---

Footnote 26:12. blessed him, and the man became great. This should be read as one
connected thought even though it has been broken into two verses.
Compare Gen 24:34, where the servant says about Isaac’s father Abraham,
“YHWH has blessed my lord very much, and he has become great.” The point
appears to be that the becoming great is the expression of divine
blessing.
