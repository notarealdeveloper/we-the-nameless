## Genesis 28:14

------------------------------------------------------------------------

¹⁴ And your seed will be like the dust of the earth, and you’ll expand to
the west and east and north and south, and all the families on the earth
will be blessed through you and through your seed.

---

Footnote 28:14. all the families on the earth. The prediction that God made to
Abraham and Isaac that all humankind will be blessed through them is now
repeated to Jacob as well. The confirmation of this prediction to each
of the three patriarchs individually highlights its tremendous
importance.
