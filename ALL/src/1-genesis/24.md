## Genesis 24:12

------------------------------------------------------------------------

¹² And he said, “YHWH, God of my lord Abraham, make something happen in
front of me today and show kindness to my lord Abraham:

---

Footnote 24:12. make something happen. The Hebrew includes the particle
n’, conveying that this is a polite request. Even as a request,
though, it is remarkable: the servant himself asks for a miraculous sign
from God, and he himself names what that sign should be. No human thus
far, including Abraham himself, has gone this far. Abraham had told this
servant that his God “will send His angel ahead of you,” and this is
presumably what gives the servant the confidence to do this. Still, it
is noteworthy as one of a chain of steps that humans are taking toward
increasing control of miracle in the Tanak.
