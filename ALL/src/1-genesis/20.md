## Genesis 20:13

------------------------------------------------------------------------

¹³ And it was when God had me roam from my father’s house, and I said to
her, This will be your kindness that you’ll do with me: to every place
where we come, say about me, “He’s my brother.”‘”

---

Footnote 20:13. had me roam. The Hebrew verb that is used in connection with God
here is a plural, which is extremely unusual. Possibly this formulation
is attributed to Abraham because he is speaking to a pagan king, and
this king has not told him that Abraham’s God has appeared to him. (The
Samaritan text has the singular verb here.)
