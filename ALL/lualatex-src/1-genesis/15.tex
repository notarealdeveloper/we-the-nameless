		And, here, YHWH’s word came to him, saying, “This one won’t take
possession from you, but rather one who will come out of your in-sides:
he will take possession from you.”

15:3–4. take possession. This term occurs three times here, emphasizing
that Abraham’s own offspring will someday take possession of everything
that is promised to him. It then occurs over sixty times in Deuteronomy
as the Israelites are about to enter the land, leaving no doubt of the
point that this promise is to be fulfilled.\\
